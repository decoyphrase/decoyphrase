# Use Cases

## 1. Secure Text Encoding for Sensitive Information

Safely convert sensitive text — such as seed phrases, private keys, access codes, or private notes — into harmless text using the Decoy Phrase Generator.

## 2. Encrypted & Permanent Backup

A secure, immutable vault for long-term storage of **Decoy Phrase data**, including the decoy text and mapping files generated by the **Decoy Phrase Generator**. Nothing sensitive is stored directly—your actual seed phrases or keys are never uploaded.

Use it to securely store:\
📄 [Decoy text](../how-decoy-phrase-works.md#id-1.-decoy-text) \
🗂️ [Mapping files](../how-decoy-phrase-works.md#id-2.-mapping-file)

## 3. Self-Custody Text File Locker

Store your own encrypted or plain text files without relying on centralized services.

* No logins tied to third-party platforms
* Accessible anytime, anywhere
* Fully owned and controlled by you — stored immutably on-chain

## 4. Secure Digital Legacy Transfer

Enable certified third parties — such as legal institutions or notaries — to assist in the future delivery of encrypted sensitive data, without ever accessing the content themselves and holding full control.&#x20;

{% hint style="danger" %}
By splitting content across multiple encrypted storage areas and delegating access through partial credentials, Decoy Phrase enables a trust-minimized, multi-party inheritance model — where no single party ever holds full control unless intentionally coordinated. [**See tips for secure legacy transfer here.**](../user-guide/common-mistake.md)
{% endhint %}
