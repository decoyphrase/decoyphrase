# About Donations

Donations help sustain the Decoy Phrase ecosystem by supporting **permanent storage**, **ongoing development**, and **community growth**.\
All donations are voluntary and non-refundable.

Decoy Phrase currently provides two donation options:

* **Storage Fund** — for permanent storage sustainability
* **Team Fund** — for team, development and ecosystem support

## Storage Fund

The **Storage Fund** is dedicated exclusively to permanent storage.

All donations sent to this fund go **directly to the storage wallet** and are **automatically added to the storage endowment**, helping sustainably fund permanent storage and increase available capacity over time.

#### How to Donate to the Storage Fund

**Option 1: Send AR from an Exchange**

1. Buy **AR** (Arweave token) on a supported exchange
2. Go to **Send** or **Withdraw**
3. Select **On-Chain Withdrawal**
4. Choose **AR (Arweave)**
5. Paste the Decoy Phrase **Arweave wallet address**
6. Select the **Arweave network**
7. Enter the amount
8. Confirm the withdrawal

> AR transfers usually take **1–5 minutes**, depending on network conditions.

***

**Option 2: Send AR from Wander Wallet**

1. Create a **Wander Wallet**
2. Buy **AR** using an available payment method
3. Click **Send**
4. Paste the Decoy Phrase **Arweave wallet address**
5. Enter the amount
6. Confirm the transaction

## Team Fund

The **Team Fund** supports the Decoy Phrase team and ecosystem, This includes technical development, infrastructure, community initiatives, **and reasonable compensation or operational support for contributors and team members actively maintaining and building the project**.

This approach ensures long-term continuity, accountability, and the ability for contributors to remain engaged and motivated in supporting the Decoy ecosystem.

When required, funds **may also be allocated to permanent storage** to ensure long-term sustainability.

#### How to Donate to the Team Fund

**Option 1: Send Stablecoins from an Exchange**

1. Buy **USDT**, **USDC**, or another supported stablecoin
2. Go to **Send** or **Withdraw**
3. Select **On-Chain Withdrawal**
4. Choose the stablecoin you want to send
5. Paste the Decoy Phrase **wallet address**
6. Select the **network that matches the wallet address**
7. Enter the amount
8. Confirm the withdrawal

{% hint style="danger" %}
Always ensure the **network and wallet address match** before confirming.
{% endhint %}

***

**Option 2: Send Tokens from a DEX**

1. Buy a **stablecoin** or the **native token** of the selected network
2. Go to **Send**
3. Select the token and network
4. Paste the Decoy Phrase **wallet address** corresponding to that network
5. Enter the amount
6. Confirm the transaction
