# Feedback & Issue Reports

We welcome feedback, bug reports, and suggestions to help improve Decoy Phrase.

To keep everything **organized, trackable, and properly followed up**, we strongly recommend submitting all feedback and issue reports through the **official form on our website**.

{% hint style="info" %}
When you submit a report through the website, your report will **automatically appear on our Discord** (for transparency and community awareness)
{% endhint %}

## How to Submit Feedback or Report an Issue

Please use the **Feedback & Support** page on our website and provide as much detail as possible, including:

* A clear description of the issue or suggestion
* Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable)
* App version, platform, or environment details
* Screenshots or reference links (optional)

Submitting reports through the website helps us track, prioritize, and resolve issues more effectively.

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Do not submit seed phrases, private keys, passwords, or any sensitive data!**
{% endhint %}

## Responses & Updates

* **Individual responses** will be sent directly to the email address you provide, using our official support email: <mark style="color:$warning;">support@decoyphrase.com</mark>
* When appropriate, **submitted feedback or issues may also be shared on our Discord server** to inform the community, track progress, or gather additional input.

{% hint style="success" %}
To protect user privacy:

* Email addresses are never displayed, published, or shared on Discord
* Any Discord post is anonymized and contains only the relevant technical or product-related context
* All email communications remain **private and confidential**
{% endhint %}

## Discord for Faster Discussion

If you prefer **faster back-and-forth discussions**, you’re welcome to join our Discord. [Join now!](https://discord.gg/q9cYTxtagJ)&#x20;

Discord is great for:

* Quick clarification
* Community discussion
* Following the progress of reported issues

However, for **official tracking, structured handling, and guaranteed follow-up**,\
**the website form remains the primary and recommended channel.**
